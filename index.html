About Janmashtami

Janmashtami is the Celebration of the birth of Lord Krishna, one of the most popular Hindu deities. It is celebrated with great devotion and enthusian across India

Celebrations

On Janmashtami, people fast, sing devotional songs,and decorate their homes with lights and flowers. The Celebration culminates with the midnight Celebration of Krishna's birth.
